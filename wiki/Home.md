## Model Overview

The DendriteModel simulates solidification microstructures using cellular automaton methods:

- **Phase-field evolution**: Tracks solid-liquid interface
- **Solute transport**: Coupled diffusion equation
- **Anisotropic growth**: Four-fold crystallographic symmetry
- **Adaptive timestepping**: Automatic stability control
